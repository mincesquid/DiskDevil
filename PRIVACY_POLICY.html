Privacy Policy for DiskDevil

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Last Updated: December 9, 2024

Introduction

DiskDevil ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how DiskDevil collects, uses, and safeguards your information when you use our macOS application.

Information We Collect

DiskDevil is designed with privacy as a core principle. We collect minimal information:

1. Local System Data (Stays on Your Device)

  • File System Analysis: DiskDevil scans your Mac's file system to identify hidden files, large files, duplicate files, and potential security issues. This data is processed locally and never transmitted off your device.
  • Security Scan Results: Information about system vulnerabilities, firewall status, Gatekeeper status, and System Integrity Protection status is collected for display but remains on your device.
  • Network Connections: When you use the Network Monitor feature, DiskDevil logs active network connections on your Mac. This information stays local and is not transmitted.
  • Privacy Settings: Your selected privacy level (1-10) and custom privacy configurations are stored locally in your Mac's preferences.

2. Subscription & Purchase Information

  • Apple Subscription Data: Subscription management is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We receive anonymized transaction receipts to verify your subscription status (Free, Premium, or Elite tier).
  • No Payment Data: DiskDevil never sees or stores your payment information. All transactions are processed by Apple.

3. Optional Analytics (Opt-In Only)

  • Usage Statistics: If you choose to enable analytics in Settings, we may collect anonymized data about:
    • Which features you use (e.g., Security Scanner, Cleanup, Privacy Slider)
    • How often you use each feature
    • Crash reports and error logs (anonymized)
    • macOS version and hardware specifications (no serial numbers or identifiers)
  • Completely Optional: Analytics are disabled by default. You can enable or disable them at any time in DiskDevil Settings.
  • No Personal Identifiers: Analytics data is anonymized and cannot be traced back to you.

4. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • File contents or document data
  • Browsing history or web activity
  • Passwords or credentials
  • Location data
  • Contacts or calendar information

How We Use Your Information

Information collected by DiskDevil is used solely to:

  • Provide App Functionality: Display security scan results, disk usage statistics, hidden files, and network connections.
  • Verify Subscriptions: Check your subscription status to unlock Premium or Elite features.
  • Improve the App: If you opt in to analytics, we use aggregated data to identify bugs, improve performance, and prioritize new features.
  • Provide Customer Support: Help troubleshoot issues if you contact us (only information you choose to share).

Data Sharing & Third Parties

  • No Data Selling: We do not sell, rent, or trade your information to third parties. Ever.
  • Apple App Store: Subscription purchases are processed by Apple. They receive transaction information as part of their payment processing. See Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Analytics Provider (If Enabled): If you opt in to analytics, anonymized usage data may be processed by our analytics provider (e.g., TelemetryDeck, which is privacy-focused and GDPR-compliant).
  • No Advertising Networks: DiskDevil contains no ads and does not share data with advertising platforms.

Data Security

  • Local Processing: All security scans, file analysis, and network monitoring happen entirely on your Mac. Data does not leave your device.
  • Secure Storage: Any settings or preferences are stored using macOS's secure UserDefaults system.
  • Encrypted Transmission: If any data is transmitted (e.g., subscription verification), it uses industry-standard TLS encryption.
  • No Cloud Backups: DiskDevil does not sync your data to the cloud. Everything stays local.

Your Privacy Rights & Controls

You have complete control over your data:

1. Manage Permissions

  • Full Disk Access: You can revoke Full Disk Access at any time in System Settings > Privacy & Security. DiskDevil will still function with limited capabilities.
  • Network Access: You can disable DiskDevil's network monitoring features at any time.

2. Manage Subscriptions

  • Cancel Anytime: Go to System Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions to cancel your Premium or Elite subscription.
  • No Data Retention: Canceling your subscription does not delete local app data (you must uninstall the app to remove data).

3. Delete Your Data

  • Uninstall: To delete all local data, simply uninstall DiskDevil. All settings, scan results, and preferences will be removed.
  • No Server Data: Since DiskDevil doesn't store data on our servers, there's nothing to delete remotely.

4. Opt Out of Analytics

  • Open DiskDevil Settings > Privacy
  • Toggle "Send Anonymous Usage Data" to OFF
  • Analytics are disabled by default

Children's Privacy

DiskDevil is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us and we will delete such information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. When we make changes:

  • The "Last Updated" date at the top will be revised
  • We may notify you through the app or via App Store update notes
  • Continued use of DiskDevil after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy

International Users & GDPR Compliance

DiskDevil processes all data locally on your device. Since we don't transmit or store personal data on servers, GDPR data transfer concerns do not apply. If you reside in the EU/EEA:

  • You have the right to access any data we hold (we don't hold server-side data)
  • You have the right to request deletion (uninstall the app)
  • You have the right to opt out of analytics (disabled by default)
  • Subscription processing by Apple is covered under Apple's GDPR compliance

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information we collect and how we use it. As stated above, DiskDevil collects minimal data and processes everything locally. We do not sell personal information.
